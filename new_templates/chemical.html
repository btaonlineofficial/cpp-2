About the Department

Overview & vision of Chemical Engineering

Chemical Engineering is a branch of Engineering where engineers convert raw materials into useful products by suitable conversion processes like distillation, absorption, adsorption, evaporation, crystallization etc. Generally Chemical Engineering is divided into three stages — the treatment of raw material, proper chemical transformation & the separation of desired chemical products.


There are a lot of chemical industries throughout the country including West Bengal. This Engineering branch deals with the essential needs of mankind — Foods, Clothing & Housing, Health, Energy Consumption, Communication, Application of Natural Sources, Protection of Environment etc.


This Diploma in Chemical Engineering course was started at Contai Polytechnic from 2008 with an intake of 60 students. After completion of Diploma Course, many students have secured jobs through Campus Interviews at companies like Haldia Petrochemicals, Mitsubishi Chemicals, ITC, Gokul Ref. Oil & Solvent Limited, Matix Fertilizers etc.

HOD's Message

From the Head of Department

⚗️
HOD's Message

"The Department of Chemical Engineering was established in the year 2008 and currently offering Diploma Program with 60 intake. The Department has been producing competent Chemical Engineers to the society right from its inception."

Students are admitted to these programmes based on the merit list of their JEXPO & VocLet examination. The department has well qualified faculty dedicated to teaching and doing research in fundamental and advanced areas.

The Department is well equipped with all the required laboratories as per the curriculum, follows a well-disciplined teaching-learning process, and maintains a win-win student-teacher relationship on campus. Students after completing the diploma program are absorbed into reputed firms through campus placements like Haldia Petrochemicals, JSPL, JSW, JSL, Mitsubishi Chemicals, ITC, Gokul Ref. Oil & Solvent Limited, Matix Fertilizers etc.

The department aims at inculcating lifelong learning skills in the students and is acting like an incubation centre in shaping the professional life of students. All the experienced and devoted faculty members are involved deeply to shape up the career of students.

Mr. Suman Kanti Gayen HOD — Chemical Engineering, Contai Polytechnic

Semester-wise Curriculum

3-Year Diploma — 6 Semesters (WBSCTE)

Semester Key Subjects
1st Semester Mathematics-I, Physics-I, Chemistry, Engineering Drawing, Workshop Practice, Communication Skills / English
2nd Semester Mathematics-II, Physics-II, Basic Electrical Engineering, Engineering Mechanics, Computer Fundamentals, Environmental Studies
3rd Semester Environmental Engineering, Computer Oriented Numerical Method, Basic Electronics, Fundamentals of Chemical Engineering, Fluid Mechanics, Chemical Engineering Thermodynamics, Environmental Engineering Lab
4th Semester Development of Life Skill-II, Process Heat Transfer, Mechanical Operation, Energy Engineering, Programming in C, Materials Science
5th Semester Mass Transfer, Chemical Reaction Engineering, Process Instrumentation & Control, Chemical Technology, Process Equipment Design, Industrial Training / Seminar
6th Semester Plant Design & Economics, Industrial Management, Project Work, Elective Subjects, Comprehensive Viva, Industrial Training

Admission Information

How to join the department

📌

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed Class X (Madhyamik or equivalent)
  • Minimum 35% marks in Science & Mathematics
  • Admission via JEXPO merit rank
  • Age: minimum 14 years at time of admission
📅

Admission Process

  • Apply through JEXPO (conducted by WBSCTE)
  • Counselling based on merit rank
  • Documents: Madhyamik marksheet, certificate, Aadhaar, photo
  • Intake: 60 students per year
