Vision & Mission

Our guiding principles

🌟 Vision

Imparting quality technical education to individuals to produce professionally highly competent, technically skilled Civil Engineers with a sense of social responsibility.

🚀 Mission

M1

To impart the fundamental knowledge required to enhance the foundation stone of a student in the field of Civil Engineering.

M2

Educate students by providing sound knowledge of environment and constructional development for preparing them to face professional challenges.

M3

Solving real life problems using technical knowledge acquired through effective teaching learning methods, contributing to the growth of society.

HOD's Message

From the Head of Department

HOD's Message

"The Civil Engineering Department was established in 1991 along with the inception of the Institute and has been playing a key role in transforming the vision of the Institute into reality for over three decades."

The syllabus of the Civil Engineering Department has been designed by the West Bengal State Council of Technical Education in such a way that a student, after passing out, can immediately become useful as a construction engineer / entrepreneur and fulfil the demand of the construction industry.

Our pass-out students are working in Central & State Govt. and prestigious industries like L&T, Gammon, Simplex, Sapoorji & Paloonji, Afcons Ltd., NCC etc. Moreover, most of the Nirman Sahayak working in Purba Medinipur District under P&RD, Govt. of West Bengal, passed out from our institution.

The Department has developed highly equipped laboratories and possesses the latest survey instruments and a very good computer facility including the latest software on CADD. Our aim will always be to ensure that present and future students enjoy all facilities that one would get at any Institute of national repute.

Mr. Abhijit Dalai H.O.D. — Civil Engineering, Contai Polytechnic

Laboratories & Facilities

Highly equipped infrastructure for practical learning

🪨

Soil Mechanics Lab

Geotechnical Engineering experiments including soil classification, shear strength & compaction tests.

💧

Hydraulics & Fluid Machinery Lab

Flow measurement, Bernoulli's theorem, pipe friction, turbines & pump experiments.

🔩

Strength of Materials Lab

Tensile, compressive, bending & torsion tests on metals and structural materials.

🏗️

Concrete & Highway Lab

Concrete mix design, slump test, compressive strength & aggregate testing for road construction.

🗺️

Survey Lab

Total station, theodolite, levelling, chain surveying with the latest survey instruments.

🌿

Environmental Engineering Lab

Water quality testing, BOD, COD analysis, sewage treatment and air quality monitoring.

Semester-wise Syllabus

3-Year Diploma — 6 Semesters (WBSCTE)

Semester Key Subjects
1st Semester Engineering Mathematics-I, Engineering Physics, Engineering Chemistry, Communication Skills in English, Basic Engineering Drawing, Workshop Practice, Fundamentals of Civil Engineering
2nd Semester Engineering Mathematics-II, Applied Mechanics, Computer Fundamentals, Building Materials, Surveying-I, Environmental Studies
3rd Semester Strength of Materials, Hydraulics, Civil Engineering Drawing, Surveying-II, Concrete Technology, Construction Technology
4th Semester Structural Engineering, Geotechnical Engineering, Transportation Engineering, Public Health Engineering, Estimating & Costing, Building Planning & Drawing
5th Semester Design of RCC Structures, Design of Steel Structures, Irrigation Engineering, Construction Management, Advanced Surveying, Quantity Surveying & Valuation
6th Semester Environmental Engineering, Railway, Bridge & Tunnel Engineering, Earthquake Engineering, CAD in Civil Engineering, Project Work, Industrial Training / Seminar

Admission Information

How to join the department

📌

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed Class X (Madhyamik or equivalent)
  • Minimum 35% marks in Science & Mathematics
  • Admission via JEXPO merit rank
  • Age: minimum 14 years at time of admission
📅

Admission Process

  • Apply through JEXPO (conducted by WBSCTE)
  • Counselling based on merit rank
  • Documents: Madhyamik marksheet, certificate, Aadhaar, photo
  • Intake: 60 students per year
