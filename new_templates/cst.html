About the Department

Overview & Mission

Department Overview

The Computer Science & Technology Department at Contai Polytechnic offers a 3-year Diploma in Computer Science & Technology under the West Bengal State Council of Technical Education (WBSCTE). The department is committed to imparting quality technical education and training students in the latest computing technologies.

The department is well equipped with modern computer laboratories, high-speed internet connectivity, and up-to-date software for practical learning. The department follows AICTE norms and is enriched with highly qualified faculty members and technical staff.

Regular industrial visits, workshops, and seminars are organized to keep students updated with industry trends. Personal attention is given to each student through a tutor-guardian relationship. Students receive hands-on training in programming, networking, database management, web development, and more.

Semester-wise Curriculum

3-Year Diploma — 6 Semesters (WBSCTE)

Semester Key Subjects
1st Semester Applied Physics-1,Mathematics-1,Applied Chemistry,Comunication Skill in English
2nd Semester Applied Physics-2,Mathematics-2,Fundamental of Electrical & Electronics,Engineering Mechanics, Introduction to IT system
3rd Semester Computer Programming In C,Scripting Languages(Python), Data Structures,Computer System & Organization, Algorithms
4st Semester Operating System,Introduction to DBMS,Computer Networks,SSAD/Software Engineering,Object Oriented Programming in Java
5th Semester MICROPROCESSOR & MICRO CONTROLLER,Internet of things,MOBILE COMPUTING,Advance Computer Network,DIGITAL IMAGE PROCESSING
6th Semester Entrepreneurship & Start-ups, Engeneering Economics & Project Management,OPEN ELECTIVE-2,Data sciences:Data warehousing & Data Mining,Cloud Computing

Higher Study Options

After completing the diploma

📝

Lateral Entry B.Tech

Diploma holders can directly enter 2nd year B.Tech (CSE/IT) through JELET in West Bengal — skipping 1st year entirely.

🏛️

Government Jobs

Eligible for SSC Junior Engineer (IT), Railway Group C/D (IT), WBPSC, and various state PSU IT posts.

🌍

Industry Apprenticeship

Register under NAPS portal or directly with IT companies for paid apprenticeship under the Apprenticeship Act.

Admission Information

How to join the department

📋

Eligibility

  • Passed Class 10 (Madhyamik) or equivalent
  • Minimum 35% marks in aggregate
  • Mathematics & Physical Science are mandatory subjects
  • Age: Minimum 14 years on 1st July of admission year
🗓️

Admission Process

  • Admission through JEXPO (Joint Entrance Exam for Polytechnic)
  • Apply online at webscte.co.in
  • Lateral entry (2nd year) through VOCLET
  • Counselling held centrally by WBSCTE
