About the Department

Overview & Mission

Department Overview

The Mechanical Engineering Department is one of the youngest departments of Contai Polytechnic, established in the year of 2010. Contai Polytechnic Mechanical Engineering Department has been providing excellent learning opportunities for diploma students. It has an intake capacity of 60 students — right now we have 64 students in First Year, 69 students in Second Year, and 60 students in Third Year class.

Mechanical Engineering remains at the core of all advancements in Engineering & Technology. The department is well developed with infrastructures & laboratories as per the AICTE norms. The dept. is enriched with highly qualified faculty members and technical staff. Regular industrial visits & trainings are organized. Personal attention is given to each student through a tutor-guardian relationship. Students of Mechanical Engineering always remain at the forefront of all activities in the institute.

It offers a 3-year Diploma in Mechanical Engineering under the West Bengal State Council of Technical Education (WBSCTE). The curriculum provides a strong foundation in thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, machine design, manufacturing processes, and CAD/CAM. Our well-equipped workshops and labs ensure hands-on experience that prepares students for industry and higher education alike.

Semester-wise Curriculum

3-Year Diploma — 6 Semesters (WBSCTE)

Semester Key Subjects
Sem 1 Engineering Mathematics-I, Engineering Physics-I, Engineering Chemistry-I, Basic Electrical & Electronics Engineering, Engineering Graphics, Workshop Practice, Communication Skills
Sem 2 Engineering Mathematics-II, Engineering Physics-II, Engineering Mechanics, Computer Fundamentals & Programming, Environmental Science, Mechanical Engineering Science, Engineering Drawing & Workshop
Sem 3 Mechanical Engineering Drawing, Mechanical Engineering Materials, Strength of Materials, Manufacturing Process-I, Thermal Engineering-I, Machine Shop Practice, Material Testing Lab
Sem 4 Theory of Machines, Manufacturing Process-II, Thermal Engineering-II, Engineering Metrology, CAD Machine Drawing Practice, Thermal Engineering Lab, Manufacturing Practice Lab
Sem 5 Power Engineering, Advanced Manufacturing Processes, Fluid Mechanics & Machinery, Elective Subjects, Power Engineering Lab, Fluid Mechanics Lab, Major Project / Internship
Sem 6 Design of Machine Elements, Work Organization & Management, Entrepreneurship & Startups, Mechatronics / Hydraulics & Pneumatics (Elective), Open Elective Subjects, Seminar, Major Project

Diploma holders can directly enter 2nd year B.Tech (Mechanical) through JELET in West Bengal — skipping 1st year entirely.

🏛️

Government Jobs

Eligible for WBPSC Clerkship, SSC JE, Railway Technician (Group C/D), and various state PSU posts.

🌍

Industry Apprenticeship

Register under NAPS / NAPS portal or directly with industries under the Apprenticeship Act for paid on-job training.

Admission Information

How to join the department

📋

Eligibility

  • Passed Class 10 (Madhyamik) or equivalent
  • Minimum 35% marks in aggregate
  • Mathematics & Physical Science are mandatory subjects
  • Age: Minimum 14 years on 1st July of admission year
🗓️

Admission Process

  • Admission through JEXPO (Joint Entrance Exam for Polytechnic)
  • Apply online at webscte.co.in
  • Lateral entry (2nd year) through VOCLET
  • Counselling held centrally by WBSCTE
