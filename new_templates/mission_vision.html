Mission & Vision

Contai Polytechnic
🎯

Our Mission

1
To offer excellent quality technical education for the fulfilment of need of industries as well as nation serving.
2
To constitute best suited outcome based teaching learning mechanism, laboratories and modern class room facilities and promote industry-institute interaction assuring world-class bench mark.
3
To inspire students in learning innovative technologies for developing new products, services and patents through a structured programme and professional skills training enhancing practical knowledge, integrity and societal value.
4
To encourage women empowerment by providing technical education for girls from remote areas and weaker families.
5
To deliver suitable vocational education & training by utilising institutionalized potential to impart entrepreneurship and professionalism among the students for betterment of their future as well as development of the society.
6
To provide quality training through Training Provider Center by well qualified faculties and create employment strengthening skilful manpower of the country.
👁️

Our Vision

Contai Polytechnic envisages of being a foremost Institute in pursuit of excellence in technical education, skill development and vocational education to serve the society in the best possible way.

Leading excellence in technical education since 1991. We are committed to producing world-class diploma engineers and shaping the innovators of tomorrow.
