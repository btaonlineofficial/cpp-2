Vocational Nodal Cell

Contai Polytechnic — Purba Medinipur Nodal Centre

🏢

About the Centre

In recent years, the Vocational Education and Training Department has been successfully imparting training to VIII+ students and technical education for X+ students across the state. In 2008, 'Nodal Centres' were established in each district to effectively monitor and migrate information to the large number of vocational centers.

Contai Nodal Centre is the largest Nodal Centre in West Bengal, situated within Contai Polytechnic. Controlled by the Directorate of Vocational Education & Training, Govt. of West Bengal, it has been operational since July 2006.

Presently, 423 Vocational Training Centres (VTCs) run throughout the Purba Medinipur District for VIII+ and X+2 levels, all affiliated with WBSCT&VE&SD.

⚡

Major Activities

VTC Management

Monitoring & supervision of 423 VTCs in Purba Medinipur District.

Examination Cell

Conducting examinations and centralized evaluation of answer scripts.

Liaison

Maintaining constant liaison among VTCs, Council, and Directorate offices.

Administration

Distributing important circulars, notices, and performing inspections.

Nodal Officer's Message

Besides Contai Polytechnic, another very important wing is the Nodal Centre. Our main objective is to monitor and supervise different Vocational Training Institutes (VTCs) for the improvement of Vocational Education. Since its inception, this Nodal Centre has played a pivotal role in the district. All VTCs, teachers, staff, students, and guardians continue to extend their helping hands toward our mission of excellence.

Dr. Prabir Kr. Maity OIC cum Nodal Officer, Contai Polytechnic
